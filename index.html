What is the debt service ratio?
annual debt service is the total payments made to pay back a loan or other debt in the span of one yearl It applies on individual debts and companies or government debt.
How to calculate debt service ratio?
To calculate debt service ratio you should use the following formula:
debt service ratio = net operating income / debt service
example: company x has $250,000 in net operating income and it also has $180,000 worth of debt service, calculate the annual debt service?
Using the annual debt service forumula the answer will be:
debt service ratio = net operating income / debt service
debt service ratio = $250,000 / $180,000
debt service ratio = 1.38
Why use debt service ratio?
The debt servise is used because it determines the cash flow that most be available in order to cover the loans and other debts.
